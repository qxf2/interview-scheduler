We have developed this application to handle large volume of candidates during our interviews. You can refer the source code here.

  • Allow us to handle a large volume of candidates without having to bother about scheduling
  • Make the interaction with Qxf2 different from other companies
  • Reduce (almost eliminate) exchange of emails between candidate and interviewer
Interview scheduler has three main entities: Jobs, Interviewers and Candidates

Adding an interviewer

  • Navigate to interviewers
  • Add your details like name, email id and your available time slots
  • You can give any number of time slots with breaks

Adding a candidate

  • Navigate to candidates
  • Add the candidates details like name, email and job applied
  • Add comments about the candidates if it's specified in their resume or in the email or if somone internally referred that candidate

Adding a job

  • Navigate to jobs
  • Add the Job with interviewers
  • Add the rounds for the job added

General instructions

  • Once the interview is scheduled we can't reschedule it through our application. We can further do it through Email. Interviewer should take care of it
  • Once the interview is done, it is interviewer's responsibility to add feedback. It will be helpful for the interviwer of the next round
  • If you are the interviewer of last round ,don't send any reject/accept to the candidate. We will take care of it
